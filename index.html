    Chapati Recipe

    Rice Recipe

Chapati is one of the favourite meals among the East African community especially Kenya. It's a no miss meal in any occassion be it formal or informal, corporate traditional or religious functions.

It's prepared from mainly wheat flour and water but for better taste we are going to learn more than the traditional ways of preparing chapati

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Wheat Flour
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 3 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 500 ml Cooking Oil
  • 500 gms Butternut

Steps

  1. In a pot brint to boil the butternut till it is soft and mashy. Set it aside.
  2. In a basin, mix the dry ingredients.
  3. Add 2 tablespoon of Oil
  4. Add the boiled butternut and with your hands intergret it with the dry ingredients
  5. Add cold/ warm water (as you may prefer) gradually as you knead.
  6. Knead until it is soft. Make it doesn't stick to your hands. you may add flour or water till you find the desired consistency.
  7. Move the dough to the table and continue kneading for 5-10 minutes. Add 2-3 tablespoon of oil as you knead.

Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups rice
  • Water
  • 3 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Frozen peas
  • carrot

Steps

  1. wash and rine the rice 3-4 time then soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Bring 4 cups of water to boil
  3. In the boiling water, grate 2 medium size carrots
  4. Add salt and oil
  5. In the boiled water add the soaked rice, let it simmer in medium heat . (cover The pot)
  6. Add the frozen peas one the rice is almost dry.
  7. After 5 minutes remove from the pot and let it rest for 10 minutes
  8. Serve and enjoy.
